# **SWR**
---------------------------------------------
The Safe Withdraw Rate is an `assumption` that users can enter on the Assumptions Page. 
The style of the `C-POP` value on the `TimelineTable` changes based on the SWR.

## **SWR on the Assumptions Page**
---------------------------------------------
The SWR Picker is a block where the user can choose their Safe Withdraw Rate. The SWR Picker:

- Has a title: "Safe Withdraw Rate"
- Subtitle: Choose your Safe Withdraw Rate
- Default: 4.0%
- Range: 0.1% - 12.0%


## **C-POP Styling**
---------------------------------------------
The styling of the values in the C-POP rows varies based on the relationship to the SWR value:

IF: the C-POP is BELOW the SWR, THEN: render the C-POP text color=green
IF: The C-POP is between SWR and SWR+1.0: THEN render the C-POP text color=orange
IF: the C-POP is greater than SWR+1.0: THEN: render the C-POP text with color=red
